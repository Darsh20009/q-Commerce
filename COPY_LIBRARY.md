## 12. Multilingual Notes

### Arabic Considerations
- Avoid direct English translations
- Use formal, respectful tone
- Cultural references matter
- Prices always shown in SAR
- Dates formatted as DD/MM/YYYY

### English Considerations
- Clear, accessible language
- Active voice preferred
- Avoid complex jargon
- Concise sentences
- Consistent terminology

---

## 13. Copy Guidelines Checklist

- [ ] No prohibited words (AI, autopilot, magic, etc.)
- [ ] Tone matches content type
- [ ] Action verbs used in CTAs (not "click here")
- [ ] Numbers formatted consistently (prices, quantities)
- [ ] Consistent capitalization (Title Case for headers)
- [ ] Active voice preferred
- [ ] Error messages are helpful, not blaming
- [ ] Success messages are celebratory but brief
- [ ] Multilingual: both Arabic and English reviewed
- [ ] Brand voice maintained throughout
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] Mobile-friendly length (not too long)
- [ ] Accessible language (8th grade reading level)
